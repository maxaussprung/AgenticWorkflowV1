# Decisions (ADRs)

Cross-cutting architecture and design decisions that span more than one product,
feature, or requirement — or that record a rejected alternative worth not
relitigating. Each decision (ADR) is referenced by the products, features, and
requirements it governs via their `decisions:` field; the back-links on this
page's entries are rendered automatically.

A decision belongs here only when it is **cross-cutting** (governs 2+ items),
carries **rejected alternatives + rationale**, or is a **technical-origin** choice
with no single "the system shall…" owner. Anything scoped to a single requirement
stays inline in that requirement's **Architecture** section instead. ADRs are
owned by the architect role.

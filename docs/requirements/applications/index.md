# Applications

The classifications below are used by the **Business Criticality**, **Technical Fit** and **Functional Fit** columns of every application page.

## Business Criticality

How critical is this application according to our business impact analysis?

| Value | Description |
|---|---|
| Business Operational | Contributing to efficient business operation but out of direct line of service to customer. |
| Business Critical | A business-critical service requires continuous availability, short breaks in service are not catastrophic. |
| Mission Critical | Breaks in service are intolerable and immediately significantly damaging. Availability required at almost any price. |

## Technical Fit

We assess technical fitness to make it trivial to identify the technical healthiness and compliance of our overall application portfolio.

| Value | Description |
|---|---|
| Inappropriate | The application violates our minimum standards and guidelines or imposes a CRITICAL risk. |
| Unreasonable | The application is insufficient per our standards and guidelines or imposes a MAJOR risk. |
| Appropriate | The application is sufficient per our standards and guidelines and imposes at most a MINOR risk. |
| Excellent | The application is excellent per our standards and guidelines and even MINOR risks are remediated, where feasible. |

## Functional Fit

The functional fitness is the minimum of Business Fit, Information Quality, Correctness and Performance.

| Value | Description |
|---|---|
| Inappropriate | The application is INAPPROPRIATE in terms of business fit, performance, correctness or information quality. |
| Unreasonable | The application is NOT GOOD ENOUGH in terms of business fit, performance, correctness or information quality. |
| Appropriate | The application is GOOD ENOUGH in terms of business fit, performance, correctness and information quality. |
| Perfect | The application is PERFECT in terms of business fit, performance, correctness and information quality. |
